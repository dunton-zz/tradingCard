Golden Retriever

A lot of people refer to Golden Retrievers as being very good dogs. But here's a fun fact, ALL dogs are good dogs

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 38 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: Your Home

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl and were named 'retriever' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged. Golden Retrievers have an instinctive love of water, and are easy to train to basic or advanced obedience standards.
